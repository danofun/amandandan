The Couple —

Amanda Miller

  • "Professional" Sleeper
  • Crazy Dog Lady
  • Barre Enthusiast

Dan Hemberger

  • Research Fiend
  • Sports Fanatic
  • Bar Enthusiast

For years, Amanda and Dan have made a habbit of telling impronptu, random stories of how they first met. Everything from hospital roommates to a parent teacher conference to FarmersOnly.com. The real story may not be quite as exciting as the one you've previously heard. The couple was introduced by a mutual friend at a barre, Liberty Street Tavern. After another group gathering, Dan mustered the courage to ask Amanda for her number. The next day, Dan sent Amanda a photo as he drove past a giant T-Rex he spotted on route 80 near Milford, Nebraska. Surprisingly, she replied and the rest, as they say, is history.

Between work, barre, the rink, and the diamond, Amanda and Dan enjoy those evening moments after they've had a chance to slow down, catch-up, and share a great meal. When they're snuggled into the couch, a book is nearby, the dog is licking his paws and the evening's possibilities seem endless yet there's noplace they'd rather be. Maybe it was inspired by those tiny front paws, maybe by that first shared photo, either way they named their aforementioned, ferocious Yorkshire Terrier, Rex.

We are thrilled to have the opportunity to celebrate our love and public commitment to each other with our closest friends and family. Thank YOU for taking the time out of your busy lives to celebrate with us!

The Proposal

"Veux-tu m'épouser?"
— Dan. Don't ask him to repeat it..


Dan proposed to Amanda while they were on vacation in Quebec City, Ontario. The evening began with a beautiful dinner followed by a stroll through the streets of Old Quebec. The two boarded a nearly empty boat to experience the beauty of Old Quebec from the water. This would have been a great place to propose, but nope, not here.. After returning to shore, the couple took a ride up the Funicular to the promenade that surrounds the east side of the Frontenac. After a short walk, the couple stopped to admire the beauty of the St. Lawrence river. Dan got to one knee and asked, "Veux-tu m'épouser?" Thankfully the custom of kneeling and holding a ring did most of the communicating as the French may have been a little rusty. Nonetheless, Amanda replied, "Yes! Oui!"
